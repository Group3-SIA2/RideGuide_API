Data Deletion Instructions

Home Privacy Policy Legal / Terms of Service Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your RideGuide account and associated personal data at any time.

Option 1: In-app request

From your account settings, submit a deletion request.

Option 2: Email request

Email support@rideguide.app with subject: Data Deletion Request, including your registered email address.

Facebook Login users

Facebook can submit an automatic deletion callback to:

https://rideguide.test/facebook/data-deletion/callback

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within a reasonable period, subject to legal retention requirements.
